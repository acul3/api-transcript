Patient: Oh, got it! Wait, what does Levitra do exactly?

Patient: I see, that makes sense. Are there any specific side effects I should watch for?

Patient: Okay, I feel a bit better about that. 

Doctor: Good! And just to clarify, it’s important to keep track of your PSA levels given your history. You should really monitor any changes in that, especially as we talk about any procedures coming up.

Patient: Definitely. I’ll make sure to keep an eye on that. 

Doctor: Great. Also, let’s schedule a follow-up appointment in, say, two weeks? That way, we can see how the increased dosage works for you.

Patient: That sounds good! I’d like to check in after I try the new dosage. 

Doctor: Perfect! I’ll have my office reach out to set that up. 

Patient: Oh, and I’ve been trying to make some lifestyle changes too—less sodium in my diet and working out more. Is there anything else I should focus on?
